5 Signs Your ETL Pipelines Are Failing

And how to fix them before stakeholders notice.

Published: May 15, 2025 | 5 min read

Sign 1: Silent Data Drift

Your dashboard numbers change slightly every month, but nobody knows why. This is usually schema drift — upstream sources adding columns or changing data types without notice.

Fix: Implement data contracts and schema drift detection. AnodGroup builds automated alerts that trigger when source schemas change unexpectedly.

Sign 2: Manual Reruns

Someone on your team manually reruns pipelines every morning. This means your jobs aren't idempotent or your error handling is broken.

Fix: Build idempotent loads with incremental ingestion. Reruns become safe and automated.

Sign 3: KPI Mismatches

Finance says one number. Product says another. Ops says a third. This is a definition failure, not a compute failure.

Fix: KPI reconciliation workshops + versioned transformation layers. One source of truth, enforced by code.

Sign 4: Backfills Are Terrifying

When you need to reprocess historical data, it's a risky, manual process that often breaks reporting.

Fix: Designed backfill strategies with partition-aware reprocessing. Reproduce any date range without duplicating records.

Sign 5: Cloud Bills Grow Faster Than Data Volume

Your Snowflake/BigQuery costs are climbing, but your data isn't. This usually means full reloads instead of incremental patterns.

Fix: Incremental ingestion, partition pruning, and compute tracking per pipeline. We make costs explainable.
