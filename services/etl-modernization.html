ETL Modernization Services

Brittle pipelines → observable, testable, cost-predictable systems. End-to-end refactoring with minimal disruption.

Why ETL Modernization Matters

We modernize brittle ETL pipelines into observable, testable, cost-predictable data workflows — so your BI dashboards stop "randomly" drifting, your finance close stops slipping, and your stakeholders stop questioning the numbers.

Legacy ETL Assessment & Roadmap

  • Sources, targets, and downstream consumers (BI, finance, ML features)
  • Failure history and incident patterns
  • Transformation hotspots (business-critical logic and edge cases)
  • Security posture (IAM, secrets, audit logs)
  • Cost drivers (compute per pipeline, full reload patterns)

ETL Re-Architecture & Refactoring

  • Idempotent loads (re-runs don't duplicate or corrupt data)
  • Incremental ingestion (watermarks, CDC, partitioning, dedup keys)
  • Versioned transformations (documented logic with code review)
  • Data contracts + schema drift detection
  • Automated testing (unit + integration checks)
  • CI/CD for pipelines (repeatable releases and rollback paths)

Performance & Cost Optimization

  • Replace full reloads with incremental patterns
  • Partition pruning for large tables
  • Compute visibility per pipeline (cost accountability)
  • Query optimization for transformations

Engagement Models

  • Consulting — Clear plan before committing to build
  • End-to-End Delivery — One accountable team to refactor, migrate, cut over, stabilize
  • Ongoing Support — Continuous hardening, cost/performance tuning

Technologies We Use

Tool-agnostic, but opinionated about reliability: Airflow, Dagster, Prefect, dbt, SQL, Python, Spark, Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Databricks, Great Expectations, and cloud-native services (AWS, Azure, GCP).
