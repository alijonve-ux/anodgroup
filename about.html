About AnodGroup

ETL engineering for organizations that need outcomes, not toolkits.

Who We Are

AnodGroup is a data engineering consultancy founded by Ehson Ehnov. We specialize exclusively in ETL — consulting, development, migration, and modernization.

Our approach is simple: build pipelines that are observable, testable, and maintainable. No heroics. No hidden dependencies. Just reliable data systems.

Our Mission

To make ETL boring again — so your team can focus on insights, not firefighting broken pipelines at 2 AM.

Our Background

Ehson holds an MS in Data Management and Analytics from the USA. He combines international training with practical experience building systems that work in challenging environments — offline, low-bandwidth, and on a budget.

Registered in Wyoming, USA. Serving clients globally.

Why "AnodGroup"?

Anode + Group. Anodes conduct current, transform energy, and resist corrosion. That's what we do with your data — transform it reliably, with resilience built in.
